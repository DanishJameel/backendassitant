## 🔄 How Does It Work? (The Process)

### **Step 1: Start with Offer Clarifier**
- Tell the AI about your product/service
- Answer 9 simple questions
- Get a clear, structured description

### **Step 2: Build Your Customer Avatar**
- Use Avatar Creator to understand your customers
- Research their struggles (Before State)
- Create success stories (After State)
- Validate everything with Avatar Validator

### **Step 3: Understand Customer Triggers**
- Use Trigger GPT to find what motivates action
- Identify the moments customers need your solution

### **Step 4: Create Your Marketing Strategy**
- Use Concept Crafter for positioning
- Generate hooks and headlines
- Build complete campaigns
- Add wildcard ideas for creativity

### **Step 5: Execute and Optimize**
- Use the outputs to create your marketing materials
- Test different approaches
- Refine based on results
